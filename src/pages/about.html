About Cloud Quiz Legends

Cloud Quiz is an AI-powered web application designed to generate custom multiple-choice questions that help users improve their knowledge on a variety of topics. Our goal is to make learning accessible, dynamic, and tailored to each user’s needs.

Built using modern web technologies and powered by artificial intelligence, Cloud Quiz enables users to test themselves intelligently — whether you're preparing for an exam, interviewing, or just want to challenge your brain.

The application is maintained and enhanced by a dedicated team of developers, designers, and AI enthusiasts who believe education should be both engaging and personalized.
